Usage Guidelines

Clear Space
Maintain minimum clear space equal to the height of the speed lines around the logo.
Minimum Size
Do not use the logo smaller than 16px. At 16px, use the simplified favicon version.
Background
Use white logo on dark backgrounds. Use black or blue logo on light backgrounds.
Don't
Don't rotate, skew, add effects, or change the logo colors outside the brand palette.
